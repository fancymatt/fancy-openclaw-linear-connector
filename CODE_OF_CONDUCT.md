# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a respectful, collaborative place for contributors of all backgrounds and experience levels.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- being respectful and constructive
- assuming good intent
- giving actionable feedback
- focusing criticism on ideas and implementation, not people

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or conduct
- deliberate intimidation or trolling
- publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
